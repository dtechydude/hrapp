How It Works

① Choose a category — Students, Teachers, or Parents. Students can also be filtered by class.
② Click any card to pick a photo for that user. The image previews instantly on the card.
③ Size is checked immediately — images over 100 KB are flagged in red and excluded from upload.
④ Click Save Photos when ready. Only valid photos are saved; errors are listed in the result summary.
⚠ Tip: Compress images to under 100 KB before uploading. Use tools like TinyPNG or Squoosh for free online compression.
